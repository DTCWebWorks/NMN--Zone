How to choose an NMN product

Most shoppers are not trying to win a supplement PhD in one afternoon. They usually just want a cleaner way to compare the basics.

Delivery format

Compare plain capsules, powders, and liposomal options. Delivery format is often one of the first forks in the road.

Dosage

Look at milligrams per serving, capsule count, and how long one bottle lasts instead of staring only at the front label.

Formula style

Some products keep things simple. Others add ingredients like resveratrol, quercetin, TMG, B vitamins, or other extras.

Brand transparency

Check the product page for manufacturing notes, testing language, and whether the company explains the formula clearly.
